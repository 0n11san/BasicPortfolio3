About Me

Hi! I'm an Army brat w/ a love of cultures; art; and philosophy (especially as it relates to politics). I dabble in web design and coding and would like to leave something significant for the world --- something that makes it a better place for many people.

If you're interested in collaborating or just want to check out my work, please drop me a line or examine any of my profiles on the linked pages.
